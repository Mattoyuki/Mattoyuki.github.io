What makes me... well... Me!

My passion growing up was always music. Music drove everything I did in my teenage years. I started learning the piano when I was six or seven years old and continued through my schooling to practice and perform. Combine this with being in the high school band, jazz band, and choir; and I would say music played a part in who I became. I learned to express myself through music because I found expression through words to be difficult. Music, however, gave me the chance to be me and show others who I was.

I am also a person who loves to experiment and try new things. I am always wanting to try new foods, hobbies, games, musical instruments, ect. Trying new things it what helps me feel like I am living my life to it's fullest.

I am currently studying computer science in the afternoon and evening. Combine this with work and my day is quite busy, but I find time for my wife and our two dogs when I'm at home. My world revolves around them. They are a part of who I am and I would not want it any other way. My past, my current interests, and my family are what make me... well... Me!
